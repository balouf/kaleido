Kaleido is a jazz group of five passionate musicians exploring modern jazz
with freshness and originality. Between covers and original compositions,
the collective pays tribute to the legendary Wayne Shorter -- with 18 of his
pieces in their repertoire including Footprints, Infant Eyes, and ESP --
while cultivating a taste for unusual harmonies and rhythms.

The band also features 7 original compositions: Jardins Suspendus, Herbie,
Spybot, and other creations that reflect Kaleido's unique musical identity.

With a repertoire of nearly 200 pieces, each concert becomes a captivating
musical experience where the worlds of each musician blend to create a
unique atmosphere.
