About

Kaleido is a jazz group of five passionate musicians exploring modern jazz with freshness and originality. Between covers and original compositions, the collective pays tribute to the legendary Wayne Shorter (Footprints, Infant Eyes, ESP and many more) while cultivating a taste for unusual harmonies and rhythms.

The band also features original compositions: Jardins Suspendus, Herbie, Spybot, and other creations that reflect Kaleido's unique musical identity.

With a repertoire of nearly 200 pieces, each concert becomes a captivating musical experience where the worlds of each musician blend to create a unique atmosphere.
